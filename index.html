The Plant Quiz App

Description

My app is a simple quiz about basic plant knowledge such as you'd be expected to know in Year 10 Science. You get a mark for every correct answer and at the end it adds up your score and tells you what you got out of 10.

Purpose

To test knowledge for students who are doing Year 10 science. Actually, I just made that up but it's just a quiz to have a bit of fun ... or not :)
